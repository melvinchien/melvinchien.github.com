About

Hello there! My name is Melvin Chien and I am a web designer from Toronto.

I specialize in improving the user interface of web applications, ensuring that people have a pleasant experience and get a lasting impression from websites, desktop and mobile applications, and other forms of software.

I am passionate about the union of human-computer interaction and software development.

I received my undergraduate degree in Informatics with a specialization in Human-Computer Interaction from the University of California, Irvine in June of 2013. I have experience in agile software development in addition to performing a variety of methods for evaluating software, including user studies, heuristic evaluations, and expert reviews.
