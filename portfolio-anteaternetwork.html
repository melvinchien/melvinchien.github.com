About

The Anteater Network is a dynamic and extensible website that connects University of California, Irvine alumni who have started their own businesses to past, present, and future students and falculty.

By displaying these businesses on a map and offering a variety of filters the Anteater Network offers a highly effective, convenient, and visual representation of the spread of UCI's alumni network.

The system was created for the UCI Alumni Association as my senior design project in the informatics undergraduate program.

Features

  • Responsive interface for compatibility with all screen sizes
  • 600+ UCI alumni owned businesses
  • User friendly school and business category filters
  • Powerful and flexible instant search
  • Toggle-able pin clustering
  • Full screen map mode
  • Buffered auto focusing map after searching
  • Custom admin panel for UCIAA staff
  • Address geocoding and caching
