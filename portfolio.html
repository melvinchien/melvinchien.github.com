Anteater Network is a new, clean, and expandable system created by JMMP for the University of California, Irvine Alumni Association as my senior design project for the informatics undergraduate program.

The system helps connect current and past students and faculty to other alumni who have started their own businesses. By displaying these businesses on a map and offering a variety of filters the Anteater Network offers a highly effective, convenient, and visual representation of the spread of UCI's alumni network.
