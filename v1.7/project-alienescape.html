About

Alien Escape is a short, original, tiled based, adventure video game developed as part of a 7 week project that involved creating prototypes, presentations, and documentation in addition to the game itself.

The player takes the role of an alien who has been captured and placed in quarantine. The alien must find an engine piece on each level to unlock the teleporter to the next level without running out of steps, all while playing under a fog of war and avoiding various enemies and traps.

Features

  • 3 different levels
  • Original artwork and sprites
  • Original music and sound effects
  • Original fog of war and line of sight algorithms
  • Original artificial intelligence algorithms
  • Multiple unique sound zones per level
  • 4 tiers of enemy intelligence
  • 2 tiers of turret intelligence
