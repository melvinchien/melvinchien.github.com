About

Terris is a gaming community that I co-founded in December 2011. The community is largely centered around Minecraft, though it has been steadily expanding to other popular games.

Formed from another Minecraft community I created in March 2011, founding Terris enabled me to expand our reach and infrastructure in the world of gaming.

During the 18 months, I actively created, managed, and directed new projects and contests for the community. In addition, I maintained all aspects of Terris including the weekly meetings, finances, social media presence, graphical identity, technical support, and in game operations.

Features

  • Over 600 registered members
  • 4 active game servers
  • 20 staff members covering all time zones
  • Large, beautifully themed worlds
  • Hand picked plugins that add depth to gameplay
  • Custom mod packs for an entirely new experience
  • Rotating daily and weekly events
  • Monthly contests and giveaways
