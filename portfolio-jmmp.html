About

JMMP was a student group formed for the year long Informatics 191 senior design project course at the University of California, Irvine. Our goal was to develop the Anteater Network system in collaboration with our sponsors, the UCI Alumni Association.

JMMP operated for in an agile software development environment for one year, with iterative bi-weekly cycles of planning, analysis, design, development, testing, and presentations.
